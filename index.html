Who am I?

Since 2014, I am a Data Science Researcher at Federal University of Sergipe. Also, I have a Bachelor in Information Systems and Master Degree (MSc.) in Computer Science at Federal University of Sergipe. In addition, I also have a PhD in Computer and Electrical Engineering at Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte.

My research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Machine Learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), Natural Language Processing (NLP), Experimentation in Computing, Graph Theory, Graph Analysis, Graph Representation Learning, Graph Machine Learning and Complex Network Analysis.

My research involves the use of LLMs to solve practical and complex problems in various scientific fields, as well as the use of Machine Learning techniques in various types of complex networks, modeled from several heterogeneous and unstructured data sources.

For more information, see my Lattes CV.
